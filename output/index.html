Recommendations

Immediate Actions

  • When thunder roars, go indoors. Seek shelter during thunderstorms.
  • Stay out of the water. Dangerous surf and current conditions are expected.
  • Stay hydrated and avoid strenuous activity during the hottest part of the day.
  • Do not drive through flooded roadways. Turn around, don't drown.
  • Avoid swimming in hazardous surf conditions and obey posted flags.
